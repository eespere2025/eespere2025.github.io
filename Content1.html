November Character Banner

Theres a new banner for this spooky november and who else is on it other than the 77th director of the “wangsheng funeral parlor” and the “housekeeper of the kamisato clan” , Hu-tao and Thoma.

Hu-tao and Thoma are not the only characters featured in this banner, there is also the “Wine industry slayer” diona kätzlein and “shuumatsuban’s resident ninja ” sayu.

Both sayu and diona are amazing 4 stars supports with thoma’s abilities to be verified.

Hu-tao, on the other hand, is an amazing dps setting records when she first came out and while she may not be the best dps anymore she’s still the best dps in most traveler’s hearts.

All this wrapped in a single banner are sure to make most spend their precious primogems for a chance for hu-tao or if you don’t want hu-tao any of the other characters
All of these characters are due to be accessible on november 2, 2021 at 3pm PST.
