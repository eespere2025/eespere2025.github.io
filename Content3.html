Noelle

Noelle, the maid-knight of the knights of Favonius. Although she wields a claymore, she is of short stature. She was bestowed the geo element by the geo archon but as geo is the opposite of anemo she does not like the element.

She has olive green eyes and lavender hair that run until her neck, she also has a braid that runs across her head and is clipped down by a shield.

As for what she wears, she has the usual maid attire with the exemption of having a priestess hat, a cravat, and many roses along with gilded white armor on her chest and a pauldron on her left shoulder.

She has a high standard of work ethics as she wants to ensure that everyone is satisfied and goes above all expectations.
